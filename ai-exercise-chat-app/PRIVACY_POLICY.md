# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction

AI Exercise Chat System ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our fitness coaching application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address, password (hashed), authentication provider details (Google, Facebook)
- **Health Data:** Age, sex, height, weight, BMI, body condition, fitness goals, exercise preferences, available equipment
- **Usage Data:** Chat messages, workout plans, assessments, progress notes

### 2.2 Automatic Information
- Device information (browser type, OS)
- Usage patterns and timestamps
- IP address (for security)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Provide personalized fitness recommendations
- Generate AI-powered workout plans and forecasts
- Track your progress over time
- Improve our service and user experience
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and ensure security

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely in **Supabase** (hosted on Amazon AWS)
- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- Access is restricted by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies
- Only you can view your own data
- We do NOT share data with third parties for marketing

## 5. Third-Party Services

### Authentication
- **Google OAuth** and **Facebook Login:** Used only for authentication. We follow their respective privacy policies.

### Database
- **Supabase:** Provides database and authentication infrastructure. See [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

## 6. User Data Deletion

You have the right to delete all your personal data at any time. To delete your account and associated data:

1. Log into your account
2. Go to **Settings** → **Delete Account**
3. Confirm deletion
4. All your data (conversations, assessments, plans, messages) will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Alternatively, email us at **support@aiexercisechat.com** with your account email, and we will process deletion within 7 days.

## 7. Data Retention

- Active user data is retained while your account is active
- Upon account deletion, data is removed within 30 days
- Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery
- We do NOT sell or share your health data with anyone

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Opt-out of certain processing
- Data portability

Contact us to exercise these rights.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of changes.
